Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload documents for a stage?

Navigate to Ongoing Projects → Stage Tracking. Click on the upload area or drag and drop your file.

What happens if my submission is rejected?

The status will show 'Revision Needed' with mentor feedback. You can resubmit from the same stage.

How do I book lab equipment?

Go to Ongoing Projects → Stage Tracking → Equipment Slot Booking section.

Can I change my project team?

Team changes require faculty approval. Raise a support ticket under 'Academic' category.

How do I request a deadline extension?

Raise a support ticket with 'Academic' category and provide your reason and proposed deadline.
